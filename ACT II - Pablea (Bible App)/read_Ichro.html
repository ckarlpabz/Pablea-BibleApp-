I Chronicles

Descendants of Judah

Chapter 4:1-23

I Chro 4:1: The sons of Judah; Pharez, Hezron, and Carmi, and Hur, and Shobal.
I Chro 4:2: And Reaiah the son of Shobal begat Jahath; and Jahath begat Ahumai and Lahad. These are the families of the Zorathites.
I Chro 4:3: And these were of the father of Etam; Jezreel, and Ishma, and Idbash: and the name of their sister was Hazelelponi:
I Chro 4:4: And Penuel the father of Gedor and Ezer the father of Hushah. These are the sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah, the father of Bethlehem.
I Chro 4:5: And Ashur the father of Tekoa had two wives, Helah and Naarah.
I Chro 4:6: And Naarah bare him Ahuzam, and Hepher, and Temeni, and Haahashtari. These were the sons of Naarah.
I Chro 4:7: And the sons of Helah were, Zereth, and Jezoar, and Ethnan.
I Chro 4:8: And Coz begat Anub, and Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel the son of Harum.
I Chro 4:9: And Jabez was more honourable than his brethren: and his mother called his name Jabez, saying, Because I bare him with sorrow.
I Chro 4:10: And Jabez called on the God of Israel, saying, Oh that thou wouldest bless me indeed, and enlarge my coast, and that thine hand might be with me, and that thou wouldest keep me from evil, that it may not grieve me! And God granted him that which he requested.
I Chro 4:11: And Chelub the brother of Shuah begat Mehir, which was the father of Eshton.
I Chro 4:12: And Eshton begat Beth-rapha, and Paseah, and Tehinnah the father of Ir-nahash. These are the men of Rechah.
I Chro 4:13: And the sons of Kenaz; Othniel, and Seraiah: and the sons of Othniel; Hathath.
I Chro 4:14: And Meonothai begat Ophrah: and Seraiah begat Joab, the father of the valley of Charashim; for they were craftsmen.
I Chro 4:15: And the sons of Caleb the son of Jephunneh; Iru, Elah, and Naam: and the sons of Elah, even Kenaz.
I Chro 4:16: And the sons of Jehaleleel; Ziph, and Ziphah, Tiria, and Asareel.
I Chro 4:17: And the sons of Ezra were, Jether, and Mered, and Epher, and Jalon: and she bare Miriam, and Shammai, and Ishbah the father of Eshtemoa.
I Chro 4:18: And his wife Jehudiajah bare Jered the father of Gedor, and Heber the father of Socho, and Jekuthiel the father of Zanoah. And these are the sons of Bithiah the daughter of Pharaoh, which Mered took.
I Chro 4:19: And the sons of his wife Hodiah the sister of Naham, the father of Keilah the Garmite, and Eshtemoa the Maachathite.
I Chro 4:20: And the sons of Shimon were, Amnon, and Rinnah, Ben-hanan, and Tilon. And the sons of Ishi were, Zoheth, and Ben-zoheth.
I Chro 4:21: The sons of Shelah the son of Judah were, Er the father of Lecah, and Laadah the father of Mareshah, and the families of the house of them that wrought fine linen, of the house of Ashbea,
I Chro 4:22: And Jokim, and the men of Chozeba, and Joash, and Saraph, who had the dominion in Moab, and Jashubi-lehem. And these are ancient things.
I Chro 4:23: These were the potters, and those that dwelt among plants and hedges: there they dwelt with the king for his work.

The genealogies of Israel and Judah

Chapter 9:1-9

I Chro 9:1: So all Israel were reckoned by genealogies; and, behold, they were written in the book of the kings of Israel and Judah, who were carried away to Babylon for their transgression.
I Chro 9:2: Now the first inhabitants that dwelt in their possessions in their cities were, the Israelites, the priests, Levites, and the Nethinims.
I Chro 9:3: And in Jerusalem dwelt of the children of Judah, and of the children of Benjamin, and of the children of Ephraim, and Manasseh;
I Chro 9:4: Uthai the son of Ammihud, the son of Omri, the son of Imri, the son of Bani, of the children of Pharez the son of Judah.
I Chro 9:5: And of the Shilonites; Asaiah the firstborn, and his sons.
I Chro 9:6: And of the sons of Zerah; Jeuel, and their brethren, six hundred and ninety.
I Chro 9:7: And of the sons of Benjamin; Sallu the son of Meshullam, the son of Hodaviah, the son of Hasenuah,
I Chro 9:8: And Ibneiah the son of Jeroham, and Elah the son of Uzzi, the son of Michri, and Meshullam the son of Shephathiah, the son of Reuel, the son of Ibnijah;
I Chro 9:9: And their brethren, according to their generations, nine hundred and fifty and six. All these men were chief of the fathers in the house of their fathers.
